## How to Use This

- Start with 10 messages, not 100.
- Send only to people who obviously do client work.
- Personalize the first sentence.
- Ask them to use a real client brief, not a fake test.
- Track replies in a simple sheet: sent, replied, tried, useful, not useful, feature request.

## Why Your Original Message Was Weak

- "Can you try once" is vague and easy to ignore.
- "Tell me if you'd use it" invites lazy politeness, not real feedback.
- "Web developer" is too broad. Employees who do not write proposals are useless for validation.

## What Good Validation Looks Like

- They paste a real client requirement.
- They compare your output against how they normally write proposals.
- They tell you what is missing, wrong, or embarrassing to send.
- At least 3 people say they would use it on live deals without you pushing them.
